Colombian Demographics

In my data science project on Colombia, I analyze demographic data and compare cities and regions across Colombia. By exploring data sets and applying statistical techniques, I uncover valuable insights and trends about population characteristics and distribution.

In this project, I compare the population sizes of different cities and regions, explore the factors that influence population growth, and look at the population distribution across the country.
