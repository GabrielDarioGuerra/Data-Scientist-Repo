Fun Facts

In addition to showcasing my projects related to data science, it is important for me to know myself personally. That's why I've created a "Fun Facts" section where you can find out interesting things about me.

I am a sports enthusiast. Sports bring me joy and excitement. My favorites are basketball, soccer, tennis, and rock climbing. These activities not only keep me physically active, but also provide opportunities for competition and friendship. Speaking of tennis, I grew up just five minutes from Roger Federer's former home, so tennis is especially close to my heart.

Another aspect of my life that made a strong impression on me was my stay in Colombia. Over the past five years, I have had the wonderful opportunity to immerse myself in the rich culture of this diverse country. I have traveled extensively, explored different regions, and have had the opportunity to call temporary residences in about 10 different cities for at least a month. This experience deepened my appreciation for the vibrant people, breathtaking landscapes and fascinating traditions that make Colombia truly unique.

I have a deep love for dogs, but I have made the responsible decision not to get one of my own at this time. I go on her trips often so it would be unfair and irresponsible to leave her alone for long periods of time. But whenever I can, I enjoy spending time with my friends' dogs. I am happy to care for them, play with them, and give them the love and attention they deserve.
