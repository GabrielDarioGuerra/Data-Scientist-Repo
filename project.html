Football Euro 2024 ML Predictions

In my Football Euro 2024 ML Predictions project, I collaborated with a team to create predictions for the 2024 Euro soccer games. By leveraging AutoML techniques, we developed models to forecast the outcomes of the matches. Through data analysis and feature engineering, we identified key factors that influence game results and used them to train the models. The project showcases the power of machine learning in predicting sports outcomes and provides valuable insights for soccer enthusiasts.
